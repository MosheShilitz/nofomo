# Product

## Register
product

## Users
Israeli tech professionals aged 25-45: developers, PMs, founders, investors. They open this on their laptop every morning as part of a news and email routine, in a bright office or home environment. They have 3-5 minutes. Their job is to stay current on AI without wasting time.

## Product Purpose
NO-FOMO.AI is a Hebrew-first daily AI news digest. It collects, analyzes, and scores AI news so Israeli tech professionals never miss what matters. Success: users open it every morning by habit, trusting it as their single AI news source.

## Brand Personality
Sharp. Reliable. Effortless.

Feels like a trusted colleague who already read everything and summarized what matters. Not a developer tool. Not a newsletter template. A premium daily briefing product.

## Anti-references
- Hacker News (dense, developer-coded, ugly)
- Generic AI newsletter purple-gradient SaaS
- Terminal and dark-mode developer tools
- RSS reader apps (feel like infrastructure, not a product)
- Anything that looks like it was built for programmers

## Design Principles
1. Morning-first clarity: every element readable at a glance before the first coffee
2. Signal over noise: importance score is the first thing the eye finds
3. Hebrew-native: right-to-left layout that feels designed for it, not translated
4. Product not portal: feels like an app someone uses, not a website someone visits
5. Effortless comprehension: hierarchy so clear that scanning costs zero cognitive effort

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA minimum. Hebrew RTL throughout. Body text 16px minimum. Meta text 13px minimum, never 12px. Touch-friendly targets 44px minimum. Color is never the sole indicator of meaning.
